# Fig.13 题注（中英文）

- 中文：全球随机无震日下的像元级误报率（False Positive Rate, FPR）空间分布图（AMSR‑2 有效纬度 −70°–70°）。采用 0.25° 像元并按 1° 聚合展示，颜色表示 FPR（0–1，三段色：绿-黄-红，0 为绿、1 为红，参考 exp2 Fig.S2）。底图为灰度地形并叠加多方向阴影（PyGMT 高质量实现或 matplotlib 回退）。可见海域（A 区）整体 FPR 较高，而内陆环境区（B–E）较低，表明环境特异阈值有效抑制背景误报。当前运行中位数：A=0.242，B=0.083，C=0.125，D=0.033，E=0.100。

- English: Global per-pixel False Positive Rate (FPR) under randomly sampled non-seismic days (AMSR‑2 coverage: −70° to 70° latitude). Using 0.25° pixels aggregated to 1° for visualization, colors denote FPR (0–1) with a green–yellow–red tricolor scheme (0=green, 1=red; consistent with exp2 Fig.S2). The basemap uses grayscale hillshaded topography (PyGMT high-quality rendering or a matplotlib fallback). Marine regions (Zone A) tend to exhibit higher FPR than inland environmental zones (B–E), supporting the efficacy of environment-specific thresholds. Current medians: A=0.242, B=0.083, C=0.125, D=0.033, E=0.100.
