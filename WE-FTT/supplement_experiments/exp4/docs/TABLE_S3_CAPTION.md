Table S3. Stratified performance (2×2) by Magnitude and Depth for M≥7.0.
We report MCC and FPR with 95% CIs. Δ values are computed relative to the overall mean (window-weighted). Strata and sample sizes: shallow (0–70 km) × M7.0–7.4: n=70; shallow × M≥7.5: n=38; deep (≥70 km) × M7.0–7.4: n=28; deep × M≥7.5: n=18. Overall mean: MCC=0.804; FPR=0.038.
